Shape Race

About The Project:

This project is a 3D racing game that has singleplayer and multiplayer support. Players have the option of choosing between three tracks which represents the difficulty levels.

About The Tracks:

This project has three tracks: Small, Medium and Large. The small track is easy and for new players. The medium track is challenging but not overly difficult. The large track is the hardest and requires skill to complete.

Controls:

This project uses the w, s, a, d, i, j, k, l, and esc keyboard keys and the left mouse button during runtime.

  • Left Mouse Button: Used to interact with the ui elements.
  • W, S, A, D Keys: Used be player one to move their car. W will move the car forward, S will move the car backward, A will turn the car left and D will turn the car right.
  • I, J, K, L Keys: Used be player two to move their car. I will move the car forward, K will move the car backward, J will turn the car left and L will turn the car right.
  • Esc Key: Used to pause the game.
